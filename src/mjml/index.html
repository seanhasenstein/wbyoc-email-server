2021 Camp Registration Now Open!

Hello Basketball Officials,

Thank you to everyone that responded to our questionnaire a few weeks back. We took your responses and came up with a great schedule for our Kaukauna Camp (June 18, 19, and 20) and Plymouth Camp (July 9, 10, and 11) weekends. Both camps will include High School 2-person and 3-person sessions, as well as Men’s and Women’s College sessions.

I will be director of the high school camps again this year and Don Baumgart and Mark Woelfel will be co-directing on the college side of things.

Our camps are for officials of all skill levels and include great classroom sessions, competitive WIAA boys varsity games, individual film studies, an experienced camp staff, and excellent networking opportunities.

Also, both camps are run in conjunction with Mark Miller Wisconsin Basketball Yearbook Tournaments that include many top boy’s varsity teams from around the state.

Based on your questionnaire responses we expect sessions to fill up very fast so be sure to reserve your spot today!

For more information and online registration go to our website at www.officialsconnection.org.

Please let me know if you have any questions.

I hope to see you at camp this summer!

Tom Rusch

WBYOC Camp Director
